Purpose

Produce enough energy from solar panels with enough energy storage to power a household refrigerator and freezer without utility power. Power will be provided to the devices via a regular extension cable supplying 110v AC electricity.

Materials

Foundation will be steal reinforced concrete pured over base rock. ~3.38 yards.

12' x 10' pad

Fooings will be 12" wide by 18" deep

Walls will be 2x4 on 16" centerlines

  • 2 2x4x144" (pressure treated)
  • 2 2x4x120" (pressure treated)
  • 34 2x4x96"
  • 2 2x4x144"
  • 2 2x4x120"

6 Roof will be 2x4 king post trusses every 24" using 3x4x5 triangle for roof.

  • 6 2x4x120" (bottum of truss)
  • 6 2x4x36" (king post)
  • 12 2x4x60" (roof slope)

All of this is programmed by hand and every attempt is made to have it support a standard web browser that can support HTML 5.1 and ECMA Script.

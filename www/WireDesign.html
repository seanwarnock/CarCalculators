Design Specifications for automotive wiring

  • Maximum voltage drop shall be calculated at 12.5% for circuits.
  • Source maximum voltage shall be calculated as 14.0v.
  • Source minumum voltage shall be calculated as 9.0v.
  • SAE J1128 is required for all engine bay wire. Probably SXL or XLPE insulation from -40c to 125c.
